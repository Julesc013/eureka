# Public Evidence Summary Contract

Status: governed by `contracts/api/evidence_summary.v0.json`.

Evidence summaries are public-safe claim summaries for result cards, response
envelopes, and future evidence-detail routes. They are not truth, rights
clearance, malware safety, or automatic acceptance.

Evidence summaries must avoid long copyrighted text, raw source payloads,
credentials, private paths, and secret values. Snippets are bounded to short
public-safe context and may be omitted.
