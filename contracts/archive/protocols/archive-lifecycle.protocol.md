# Archive Lifecycle Protocol

Status: draft placeholder

This document sketches the phases that Eureka will need to coordinate without declaring the final wire format or runtime behavior.

## Intended Phases

1. capture or observe candidate material
2. identify the subject and its representations
3. record claims and evidence
4. evaluate compatibility and preservation state
5. materialize snapshots or reconstruction outputs

## Current Limits

- The phase list is descriptive, not normative.
- Transport format, sequencing rules, and trust semantics remain open.
- Connectors may participate in capture and observation, but they do not define canonical meaning.
