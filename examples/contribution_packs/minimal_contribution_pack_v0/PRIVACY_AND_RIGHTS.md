# Privacy And Rights

This synthetic contribution pack is not rights clearance, not malware safety,
and not canonical truth. The contribution records are review candidates only.

No executable files, installers, raw binaries, raw SQLite databases, raw cache
files, credentials, private local paths, private files, or long copyrighted text
are included. The pack does not grant artifact distribution permission or
download/install permission.

Future public or master-index acceptance would require governed review,
redaction, source/evidence comparison, conflict handling, takedown posture, and
operator approval.
