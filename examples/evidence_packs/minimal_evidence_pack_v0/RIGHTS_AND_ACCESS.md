# Rights And Access

This example evidence pack is synthetic and self-authored for contract testing.

- Evidence claims are not rights clearance.
- Metadata and snippets are not artifact distribution permission.
- Snippets are short, synthetic, and public-safe.
- No executable, installer, archive, binary artifact, private file, credential,
  or raw copyrighted long-form text is included.
- Future public or master-index acceptance would require a separate review
  workflow; this example does not create that workflow.
