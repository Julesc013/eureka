# Privacy And Rights Policy

Contribution packs must declare privacy and rights posture. Shareable packs must
not contain private local paths, credentials, raw private files, raw cache
exports, raw SQLite databases, executable payloads, restricted records, or
unreviewed external observations.

A contribution is not rights clearance, not malware safety, and not canonical
truth. Future hosted or master-index acceptance requires separate review and
policy gates.
