# Risks And Limitations

- Contribution packs can carry misleading claims if future review is weak.
- References to source, evidence, and index packs do not prove those packs are
  valid, public-safe, or accepted.
- Manual observations remain pending unless valid human-entered evidence exists.
- No contribution upload, import, review queue runtime, moderation UI, accounts,
  identity, or automatic acceptance exists.
- This contract does not create rights clearance, malware safety, canonical
  truth, production readiness, or source trust.
