# No Global Absence And No Mutation Policy

Known absence explanations must not claim exhaustive search and must not mutate master, public, local, candidate, source-cache, evidence-ledger, result-cache, miss-ledger, search-need, or probe-queue state.

P66 is contract-only. It adds no runtime known absence pages, no telemetry, no persistent query logging, no live probes, no source cache or evidence ledger runtime, no candidate promotion, and no public/master/local index mutation.
