# Remaining Blockers

Runtime extraction remains blocked by design until:

- sandbox policy is reviewed and approved
- concrete resource limits are approved
- executable payload risk handling is approved
- OCR/transcription policy is approved
- source-cache/evidence-ledger runtime approvals exist
- candidate-index and promotion policies explicitly allow extracted candidates
- operator approval exists

Hosted deployment remains outside P95 and unverified unless existing deployment evidence says otherwise.
