# Rights Risk And Provenance Policy

Deep extraction preserves provenance and source context.

The contract requires:

- source context references
- extraction policy references
- review-required candidate outputs
- conflict preservation
- rights clearance not claimed
- malware safety not claimed
- payload safety unknown
- manual/human review before promotion

Manifest metadata, member names, OCR hooks, and package/source metadata are observations, not rights clearance or truth.
