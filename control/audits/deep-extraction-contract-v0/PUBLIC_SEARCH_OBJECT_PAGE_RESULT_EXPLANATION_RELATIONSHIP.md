# Public Search Object Page Result Explanation Relationship

Public search may later show reviewed member, manifest, and extraction-gap summaries.

Object pages may later show representations and members. Comparison pages may compare extracted member summaries. Search result explanations may cite extraction gaps and limitations.

Deep extraction is not required for baseline search. Failed or absent extraction must be explained as a gap, not hidden. Public routes must never trigger live extraction or arbitrary file/source fetching.
