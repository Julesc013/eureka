# Synthetic Record Generation Boundary

Future extraction may produce synthetic record candidates for members, manifests, and text summaries.

Rules:

- candidates are provisional
- candidates require source/evidence references
- candidates are labelled `generated_from_extraction_future`
- candidates do not mutate the master index
- candidates do not become truth without review
- candidate index mutation requires separate approval

P95 creates only synthetic example summaries, not source-cache, evidence-ledger, or candidate-index records.
