# Near Miss And Weak Hit Model

Near misses summarize result references that almost matched but failed for a
specific reason such as wrong version, wrong platform, wrong artifact type,
parent bundle only, documentation only, weak identity match, missing member
evidence, missing compatibility evidence, source unverified, or unknown.

Weak hits summarize result references that matched weakly because of low score,
insufficient evidence, wrong lane, vague identity, missing version, missing
platform, missing member, missing actionability, or unknown.

Both models are summary-only and do not copy raw result payloads.
