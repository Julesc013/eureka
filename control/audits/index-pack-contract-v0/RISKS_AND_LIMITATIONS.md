# Risks And Limitations

Known limitations:

- The example pack is tiny and synthetic.
- The contract does not define merge semantics.
- The contract does not define trust ranking or truth selection.
- The contract does not define production signing.
- The contract does not define hosted ingestion.
- The contract does not define raw SQLite import.
- Query examples are guidance only and do not promise ranking quality.

These limitations are intentional for v0. They keep index packs safe while the
source/evidence/contribution path is still being designed.
