# Privacy And Rights Policy

Index Pack Contract v0 uses the privacy classes shared by source and evidence
packs:

- `public_safe`
- `local_private`
- `review_required`
- `restricted`
- `unknown`

Shareable and submitted packs must not contain private absolute paths,
credentials, raw private cache data, raw databases, restricted records, or
local-private records.

Index metadata is not rights clearance. Indexed existence does not imply
artifact distribution permission. The contract makes no malware safety claim.
Future public or master-index acceptance requires separate review.
