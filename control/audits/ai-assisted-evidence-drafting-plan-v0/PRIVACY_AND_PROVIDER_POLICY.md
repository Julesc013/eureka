# Privacy And Provider Policy

All providers are disabled by default. Remote providers are disabled by default
and cannot process private data by default. Local providers also require a
future explicit runtime milestone.

The plan adds no provider loading, credentials, prompt logging runtime,
telemetry, model execution, browser AI, embeddings, vector search, reranking,
or AI extraction runtime.

Committed examples are synthetic and public-safe. Private paths must be
redacted before public or committed output. Credential values must never be
sent, logged, stored, or committed.
