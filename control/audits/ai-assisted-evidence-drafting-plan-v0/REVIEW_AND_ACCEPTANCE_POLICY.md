# Review And Acceptance Policy

All AI-assisted outputs require review. Typed AI output validation is a gate,
not acceptance.

Evidence pack inclusion, contribution pack inclusion, local staging, public
search visibility, local index candidate generation, and master-index review
queue export all remain separate future workflows. No automatic acceptance is
allowed.

AI output cannot decide canonical truth, source trust, rights clearance,
malware safety, identity merge, public-search mutation, local-index mutation,
or master-index acceptance.
