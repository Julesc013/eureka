# Screenshot and Evidence Attachment Policy

Screenshots are optional unless a future schema explicitly requires them.

If a human operator attaches screenshots or evidence:

- Do not include private account, session, browser-profile, search-history, or notification data.
- Crop or redact private data before committing.
- Use repo-approved relative attachment references only.
- Do not include private absolute local paths.
- Do not include raw payload dumps, binaries, installers, or copyrighted archives.
- Do not use screenshots as a substitute for structured fields.
- Treat screenshots as evidence of what one operator saw at a time, not as truth.

If redaction is needed but cannot be done safely, leave the screenshot out and record a textual limitation.
