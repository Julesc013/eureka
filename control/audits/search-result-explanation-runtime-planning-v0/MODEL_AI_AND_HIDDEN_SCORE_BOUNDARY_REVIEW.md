# Model, AI, And Hidden-Score Boundary Review

P106 authorizes none of the following:

- model calls
- AI-generated answers
- private-chain or hidden-data explanations
- hidden scores
- hidden suppression
- result suppression
- secret scoring models
- telemetry ranking signals
- user-profile signals
- ad signals

Future model assistance would require a separate approval, typed output
validation, privacy review, no-hidden-score review, and public-search safety
review. It is out of scope for the P106 plan.
