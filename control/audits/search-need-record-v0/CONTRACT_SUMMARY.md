# Contract Summary

P62 introduces `contracts/query/search_need_record.v0.json` and
`contracts/query/search_need_lifecycle.v0.json`.

The contract says a search need is a scoped, reviewable unresolved need that may
later aggregate privacy-filtered observations, cache summaries, and miss ledger
entries. It is not a result, miss entry, probe job, candidate record, accepted
evidence, public demand count, or master-index truth.

P62 is contract/example/validation only.
