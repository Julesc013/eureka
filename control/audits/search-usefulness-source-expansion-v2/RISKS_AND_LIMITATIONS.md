# Risks And Limitations

- Fixtures are tiny, synthetic, or safely paraphrased metadata records.
- Fixtures are not live source observations.
- External baselines remain pending manual observation.
- Review-description fixtures are subjective evidence, not canonical truth.
- Manual/document fixtures do not include real copied book or magazine text.
- Wayback/Memento fixtures do not perform replay fetches or live archive calls.
- Software Heritage fixtures use SWHID-like metadata tokens only.
- SourceForge/package-registry fixtures do not expose download URLs or executable payloads.
- Compatibility evidence is bounded and partial.
- Public search remains local/prototype `local_index_only`.

No malware-safety claim, rights-clearance claim, production relevance claim, or hosted-search claim is made.
