# Source, Evidence, And Rights Policy

Candidate records can point to candidate evidence refs, source refs, source
pack refs, evidence pack refs, contribution pack refs, or future source-cache
refs. These refs are not accepted evidence by themselves.

P64 examples keep:

- live source calls false
- live probes false
- downloads false
- installs false
- execution false
- rights clearance not claimed
- malware safety not claimed

Rights and risk review remain required before any future promotion path.
