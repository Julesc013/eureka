# Evidence And Review Policy

AI output is a suggestion or candidate. It is not evidence truth and cannot
accept itself.

Typed outputs should link to source or evidence references where possible.
Unsupported claims require review. Identity, compatibility, alias, source
matching, and absence candidates must preserve limitations and provenance.

Master-index acceptance remains a separate review-queue decision with
provenance, conflict handling, privacy review, rights review, risk review, and
publication limitations.
