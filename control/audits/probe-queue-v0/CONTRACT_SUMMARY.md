# Contract Summary

Probe queue items are future work requests derived from privacy-filtered search
needs and related query-intelligence refs. They describe what a later approved
manual observation, source-cache probe, metadata probe, deep extraction, or
parser improvement might do.

P63 defines only the schema, taxonomy, examples, validation, and review
posture. Probe queue items are not evidence, candidates, connector execution,
runtime queue entries, source truth, or master-index truth.
