# Poisoning Risk Taxonomy

Poisoning risks include spam query, repeated fake demand, source stuffing, candidate poisoning, malicious identifier stuffing, prompt-injection-like text, policy bypass, live-probe forcing, arbitrary URL fetch forcing, download/install/execute forcing, source reputation attack, rank manipulation, near-duplicate flood, unsupported parameter abuse, future bot-like pattern, and unknown risk. High or critical poisoning risk blocks public aggregate eligibility.
