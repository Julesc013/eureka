# Relation To Packs And Review Queue

Typed AI outputs may later help draft evidence or contribution candidates, but
they do not enter evidence packs or contribution packs automatically.

Future pack or review workflows must preserve:

- provider ID and version
- task and output type
- source/evidence refs where possible
- limitations
- required-review status
- validation report provenance

The master-index review queue remains the future governance path for public
acceptance. Typed output validation is a pre-review shape and safety check, not
queue acceptance.
