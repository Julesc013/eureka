# Privacy And Rights Policy

Evidence packs classify privacy as `public_safe`, `local_private`,
`review_required`, `restricted`, or `unknown`. The example is `public_safe`.

Shareable packs must not include credentials, private local paths, private cache
exports, restricted snippets, executable payloads, raw private files, or raw
copyrighted long-form text.

Evidence is not rights clearance. Metadata and claims are not artifact
distribution permission. Snippets may still be subject to source rights.
