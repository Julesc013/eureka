# Privacy And Rights Policy

Source Pack Contract v0 classifies pack privacy as:

- `public_safe`
- `local_private`
- `review_required`
- `restricted`
- `unknown`

The safe default for future pack creation is local-private until validation and
explicit export/submission. A shareable pack must not contain credentials,
private local paths, raw private files, or private cache dumps.

Source packs may carry metadata and evidence notes, but they do not prove
rights clearance, malware safety, artifact redistribution permission, or
license compatibility. Rights/access notes are required, and future binary
artifacts require separate policy work.
