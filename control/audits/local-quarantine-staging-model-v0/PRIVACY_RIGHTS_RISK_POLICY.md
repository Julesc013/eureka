# Privacy, Rights, And Risk Policy

Default visibility is `local_private`.

Requirements:

- `public_safe` only after validation and review posture are recorded
- rights uncertainty remains `review_required`
- private path, credential, checksum, schema, raw database, raw cache, or
  executable risk means quarantine or rejection
- executable payloads are not staged by default
- raw database and raw cache files are not staged by default
- no rights clearance claim
- no malware safety claim
- no telemetry by default

Staging success is not canonical truth, public-search eligibility, or
master-index acceptance.
