# Acceptance Requirements

Future acceptance requires structure validation, checksum validation, pack
validator results, privacy review, rights review, risk review, source/evidence
provenance, conflict recording, and a manual or policy-governed decision.

Acceptance does not prove rights clearance, malware safety, or canonical truth.
Raw binaries, credentials, private paths, raw caches, and unsupported executable
payloads remain prohibited unless future policy explicitly changes that.
